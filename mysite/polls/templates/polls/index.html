About

This Quote generator works thanks to Markov chain. It works from a database of 78 Quotes and basically "combines" the Quotes to create a new quote. It starts by randomly picking the first word of the newly generated quote from all of the first words in the database quotes, then it looks at all the words that have appeared after that "first" word and randomly picks another word. To read more about this topic click on Markov picture. Sadly the chances of generating a quote that is actually good, are about 5/100. So do not take this website too seriously.
